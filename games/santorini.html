How to Play Santorini

Objective

Move one of your workers to the third floor (level 3) to win! Outsmart your opponent by controlling the board and building towers strategically.

The Game Setup

You play on a 5x5 grid. Each player controls 2 workers. Place your workers strategically at the start of the game.

Game Flow - Each Turn Has 2 Steps

  • Step 1 - Opponent Builds: Your opponent must build one level (or dome) on an empty square next to their workers
  • Step 2 - You Move & Build: Move one of your workers to an adjacent square, then build on another adjacent square

Movement Rules

  • Your workers can move to adjacent squares (up, down, left, right, or diagonally)
  • Workers can move UP one level, stay at the same level, or move DOWN any number of levels
  • Workers CANNOT move to squares occupied by opponent workers
  • Workers CANNOT move to domed squares (completed towers)
  • Workers CANNOT move UP more than 1 level

Building Rules

  • Towers have 4 levels: 0 (ground), 1, 2, 3
  • After level 3, you place a DOME on top, which blocks further building
  • You can only build on empty squares adjacent to the worker you just moved
  • You build one level UP from the current level (unless it's already at level 3, then add a dome)

Winning

You win immediately when one of your workers moves up to level 3! That's the winning condition - get there first!

Strategy Tips

  • Plan ahead - build towers to create higher paths for your workers
  • Block your opponent by building near their workers
  • Use domes strategically to restrict movement
  • Spread your workers to control more territory
  • Always remember: opponent builds first, then you move and build
