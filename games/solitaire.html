How to Play Solitaire (Klondike)

Objective

Move all cards to the Foundation piles (the four piles on the right) in order from Ace to King by suit. If you get all cards there, you win!

The Layout

  • Stock Pile: The deck of remaining cards on the left
  • Waste Pile: Next to stock - cards you've drawn but can't use yet
  • Foundation Piles: The four piles on the right where you build sequences
  • Tableau: The seven columns in the middle where you play

Building in the Tableau

  • Build DOWN by alternating colors (red and black)
  • Move a King to an empty column
  • Move any card on top of a card that's 1 rank higher and opposite color
  • You can move a group of cards together if they're in correct sequence
  • Example: Place a red 5 on a black 6

Foundation Piles

  • Build UP from Ace to King in the same suit
  • Only Aces can start a foundation pile
  • Once a card is in the foundation, you're done with it
  • Each foundation pile ends with a King

Using the Stock Pile

  • Click the Stock pile to draw cards
  • You can draw 1 or 3 cards at a time (check your game settings)
  • Only the top card in the Waste pile is available to play
  • When you run out of stock, click it again to recycle - you get limited passes through!

Strategy Tips

  • Try to uncover face-down cards in the tableau
  • Move cards to the foundation only when necessary
  • Keep columns open for moving sequences around
  • Don't move cards to foundations too early if you might need them
  • Use the stock pile wisely since you have limited passes
