How to Play Damme (Checkers)

Objective

Capture all of your opponent's pieces or block them so they cannot move!

Game Setup

Each player starts with 12 pieces on an 8x8 board. Players are randomly assigned red or white pieces.

  • Red pieces start on the top three rows
  • White pieces start on the bottom three rows
  • Pieces are placed only on dark squares

How to Play

Moving:

  • Regular pieces can only move diagonally forward one square
  • Drag your piece to a highlighted adjacent square to move

Capturing:

  • If an opponent's piece is diagonally adjacent and the square beyond it is empty, you must jump over it to capture
  • Captured pieces are removed from the board
  • If you can capture, you must capture (forced jump rule)
  • You can make multiple jumps in one turn if available

Kings:

  • When a piece reaches the opposite end of the board, it becomes a king (shown with a crown)
  • Kings can move and capture diagonally in both forward and backward directions

Winning

You win when:

  • You capture all of your opponent's pieces
  • Your opponent has no legal moves available

Strategy Tips

  • Try to get your pieces to the opposite end to become kings
  • Control the center of the board
  • Look for double jumps to capture multiple pieces
  • Protect your back row to prevent opponent kings
