How to Play Isola

Objective

Move your piece while removing squares from the board. The last player with a legal move wins! Your opponent will eventually be trapped.

The Board

You play on a 6x8 grid. Each player starts on opposite corners. You can only move to adjacent squares (up, down, left, right, or diagonally). Diagonal moves are allowed!

How to Play

  • Step 1 - Move: Click an adjacent square to move your piece there
  • Step 2 - Remove: After moving, click a square to remove it permanently
  • You cannot move to or remove a square that's already been removed
  • You cannot move to a square occupied by your opponent

Important Rules

  • You MUST move every turn (if you have any legal moves)
  • After moving, you MUST remove a square
  • You cannot remove the square you just moved to
  • Strategic removing: Remove squares to trap your opponent!

Winning

You win when your opponent has no legal squares to move to. This usually happens when you've strategically removed squares around them.

Strategy Tips

  • Don't just move randomly - think about limiting your opponent's options
  • Remove squares near your opponent to trap them
  • Try to keep multiple escape routes open for yourself
  • Control the center of the board when possible
  • Think ahead - your removed squares can't be used for escape later
