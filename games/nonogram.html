How to Play Nonogram

Objective

Solve the logic puzzle by filling in the correct cells to reveal the hidden picture.

Rules

  • Read the Clues: Numbers indicate consecutive filled cells in each row and column
  • Solve Logically: Use the clues to deduce which cells must be filled or empty
  • Complete the Grid: Fill all required cells to complete the puzzle

Controls

  • Click: Toggle between empty, filled, and marked states
  • Filled (Blue): Cells you think are part of the picture
  • Marked (âœ•): Cells you think are empty
  • Empty (Gray): Unknown cells

Win Condition

Fill all cells correctly according to the clues to complete the puzzle and win!

Difficulty Levels

  • Easy: 5x5 grid - Perfect for beginners
  • Medium: 10x10 grid - For intermediate players
  • Hard: 15x15 grid - Challenge yourself!
